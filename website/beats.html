My Beats

This page showcases a collection of my favorite music genres, featuring the rhythms, melodies, and artists that inspire me. Dive into the sounds that shape my musical taste and bring me joy.

OPM K-Pop K-OST Pop

OPM

Palagi

Artist: TJ Monterde, KZ Tandingan

Album: Palagi

A acoustic love song that explores the ups and downs of a relationship, emphasizing the commitment to always return to each other despite challenges.

Mundo

Artist: IV of Spades

Album: Mundo

An pop-rock anthem that explores the theme of finding solace, belonging, and love in someone else, emphasizing the idea of being each other's world.

Tingin

Artist: Cup of Joe, Janine Teñoso

Album: Tingin

A song about a strong, unwavering love that endures amidst chaos, focusing on the joy and security of a deep connection that transcends external distractions.

Pasilyo

Artist: SunKissed Lola

Album: Pasilyo

A romantic wedding and waltz ballad, exploring the theme of finding a "sure thing" in a world of uncertainty, with lyrics emphasizing the importance of a partner.

Mahika

Artist: Adie, Janine Berdin

Album: Mahika

A kilig-inducing, acoustic ballad with a contemporary pop sound, inspired by the feeling of falling in love unexpectedly and experiencing a sense of enchantment.

Sa'yo

Artist: Silent Sanctuary

Album: Monodramatic

A heartfelt rock ballad expressing deep emotional vulnerability and unrequited love, showcasing the band's signature melancholic sound.

K-pop

Gee

Artist: Girls' Generation

Album: EP (Extended Play)

A bubblegum pop track that became a defining song for the group and K-pop, known for its catchy melody, upbeat tempo, and lyrics about the feelings of falling in love.

View

Artist: SHINee

Album: View - The 4th Album

A song about embracing and appreciating the beauty of the world around us, featuring deep house and pop elements, and exploring how love intensifies our senses.

Psycho

Artist: Red Velvet

Album: 'The ReVe Festival' Finale

A contemporary R&B-meets-pop track that delves into the intricacies of a complicated romantic relationship, showcasing the fluctuating emotions and dynamics within a couple.

Love Shot

Artist: EXO

Album: Don't Mess Up My Tempo

A pop dance track with a catchy chorus and heavy 808 bass that explores the desire to rediscover true love in a world that seems to have forgotten it.

How You Like That

Artist: Blackpink

Album: The Album

An EDM, hip hop, trap, club and pop song about "not being daunted by dark situations and to not lose the confidence and strength to stand up again".

Blueming

Artist: IU

Album: Love Poem

A pop-rock song exploring the themes of modern-day romance, online interactions, anxieties, and the feeling of "blooming" with an unattainable relationship.

K-OST

Everytime

Artist: Chen, Punch

Album: Descendants of the Sun OST

An OST track featuring a medium-tempo R&B-inspired pop track with a mellow tempo and a dreamy sound, expressing a longing for the person they love.

Beautiful

Artist: Crush

Album: Goblin OST

An OST that contrasts the beauty and joy of life with the sorrow and challenges one may face, depicting life as a mix of beautiful moments and difficulties.

Here I Am Again

Artist: Baek Yerin

Album: Crash Landing On You OST

An OST expressing the themes of longing, second chances, and the enduring nature of love, reflecting the emotional journey of the characters in the drama.

Say Yes

Artist: Loco, Punch

Album: Moon Lovers OST

A soundtrack from the K-drama "Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo" that best expresses the heart of love and is a song that contains the contents of confession.

Perhaps Love

Artist: Howl, J.ae

Album: Princess Hours OST

An iconic K-drama OST known for its romantic and melancholic melody, fitting the drama's themes of love, fate, and the challenges of royalty.

It's You

Artist: Henry

Album: While You Were Sleeping OST

A romantic ballad written for the popular Korean drama "While You Were Sleeping" that express the feeling of falling in love and being captivated by someone special.

Pop

Night Changes

Artist: One Direction

Album: Four

A song about the passage of time, the bittersweet transition from youth to adulthood, and the enduring power of love amidst life's constant changes.

Perfect

Artist: Ed Sheeran

Album: ÷ (Divide)

A ballad about finding love and building a life with someone, inspired by the artist's relationship with his wife, and their rekindled connection from childhood.

Cruel Summer

Artist: Taylor Swift

Album: Lover

A song about a fleeting, passionate, and potentially risky summer romance, characterized by a sense of yearning and the thrill of a forbidden connection.

Just The Way You Are

Artist: Bruno Mars

Album: Doo-Wops & Hooligans

A pop song that expresses admiration and love for someone, emphasizing that the singer appreciates the person exactly as they are, without wanting any changes.

Yellow

Artist: Coldplay

Album: Parachutes

An alternative rock/pop rock song that explores the concept of devotion, where the narrator is willing to do anything for someone they care deeply about.

Call Me Maybe

Artist: Carly Rae Jepsen

Album: Curiosity

A pop song about the initial, unexpected infatuation of "love at first sight," where the singer, feeling overwhelmed by a sudden attraction, gives her number.
