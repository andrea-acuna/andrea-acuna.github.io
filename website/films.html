My Kind of Films

This page showcases my favorite films. Each category includes embedded video clips, offering a glimpse into the most thrilling moments, iconic scenes, and masterful storytelling that make these films unforgettable.

Romcom Filipino Animated Others

Romcom

Notting Hill

Director: Roger Michell

Year: 1999

A chance encounter brings together reserved bookstore owner William Thacker and Hollywood icon Anna Scott, who forge an improbable romance.

10 Things I Hate About You

Director: Gil Junger

Year: 1999

A high-school boy, Cameron, cannot date Bianca until her anti-social older sister, Kat, has a boyfriend. So, Cameron pays a mysterious boy, Patrick, to charm Kat.

How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

Director: Donald Petrie

Year: 2003

Ben bets his coworkers that he can make a woman fall in love with him in just 10 days. But along comes Andie, a writer with her own agenda.

13 Going On 30

Director: Gary Winick

Year: 2004

Jenna Rink wishes she could just grow up after being humiliated at her 13th birthday party — only to wake up as a 30-year-old fashion magazine editor.

The Proposal

Director: Anne Fletcher

Years: 2009

Margaret, a Canadian-born, successful book editor in New York faces deportation, she convinces her assistant Andrew to marry her in return for a promotion.

About Time

Creator: Richard Curtis

Years: 2013

When Tim learns that the men in his family can travel in time and change their own lives, he decides to go back and win the woman of his dreams.

Filipino

Got 2 Believe

Director: Olivia Lamasan

Year: 2002

A cynical wedding photographer strikes a deal with a hopeless romantic wedding coordinator. As she searches for Mr. Right, they unexpectedly fall for each other.

A Very Special Love

Director: Cathy Garcia-Sampana

Year: 2008

Editorial assistant Laida Magtalas finds herself working for the man of her dreams, Miguel Montenegro, but remains blind to some hard facts.

Seven Sundays

Director: Cathy Garcia-Sampana

Year: 2017

A father diagnosed with cancer asks his busy and grown children to spend their Sundays with him, forcing his family to confront their issues before it's too late.

Hello, Love, Goodbye

Director: Cathy Garcia-Sampana

Year: 2019

Two overseas Filipino workers living in Hong Kong intertwine and find themselves falling in love, but they each have different plans for their future.

Isa Pa with Feelings

Director: Prime Cruz

Year: 2019

When an aspiring architect learns sign language from her deaf neighbor, they find solace in each other but face challenges as their relationship grows.

Rewind

Director: Mae Cruz-Alviar

Year: 2023

When his wife dies while their marriage is in tatters, a man makes a strange deal sending him to the past to turn it around — in exchange for his life.

Animated

Spirited Away

Director: Hayao Miyazaki

Year: 2001

A young girl and her parents stumble into a magical world of spirits and gods, where she must work at a bathhouse to save her parents and return to the human world.

Monsters, Inc.

Director: Pete Docter

Year: 2001

In a world where monsters generate power by scaring children, two monsters discover that laughter is actually more powerful than screams.

Up

Director: Pete Docter

Year: 2009

Carl Fredricksen, a widower, embarks on a journey to the South American wilderness to reach Paradise Falls, a place his late wife Ellie dreamed of visiting.

Tangled

Director: Nathan Greno, Byron Howard

Year: 2010

A reimagining of the Rapunzel fairy tale, following a lost princess with magical long hair who yearns to leave her tower and explore the world.

Toy Story 3

Director: Lee Unkrich

Year: 2010

Andy, now a teenager, is about to head off to college, Woody, Buzz, and the rest of the toys find themselves accidentally donated to a daycare center.

Frozen

Director: Chris Buck, Jennifer Lee

Year: 2013

Anna embarks on a journey with Kristoff, Sven, and Olaf, to find her sister Elsa, whose icy powers have trapped their kingdom in eternal winter.

Others

The Princess Diaries

Director: Garry Marshall

Year: 2001

A heartwarming coming-of-age story about a shy teenager who discovers she is the heir to a European throne, navigating royalty, self-confidence, and family.

A Moment to Remember

Director: John H. Lee

Year: 2004

Su-jin and Chul-soo's marriage is put to test when Su-jin is diagnosed with a rare form of Alzheimer's disease, causing her to gradually lose her memories.

The Notebook

Director: Nick Cassavetes

Year: 2004

Two young lovers, Allie and Noah, are torn apart by war and class differences in the 1940s in this adaptation of Nicholas Sparks's bestselling novel.

Pride & Prejudice

Director: Joe Wright

Year: 2005

Headstrong Elizabeth Bennet meets the aloof and arrogant Mr. Darcy, they must overcome their first impressions to find love in Regency-era England.

High School Musical 3

Director: Kenny Ortega

Year: 2008

With graduation approaching, East High seniors, along with the rest of the Wildcats, stage a spring musical to create a lasting memory of their time together.

Miracle in Cell No. 7

Director: Lee Hwan-kyung

Year: 2013

A mentally-ill father is falsely accused of a crime and sent to prison, where he forms a bond with his fellow inmates, who in return help him see his daughter again.
